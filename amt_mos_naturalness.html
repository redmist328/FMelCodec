Part 1: Naturalness MOS

You will listen to speech samples generated by different voice conversion systems. For each sample, please judge how natural and human-like the speech sounds. Please focus only on the naturalness of the speech.

Please rate each sample on a 1-5 scale:

  • 1 = Bad: very unnatural, severely distorted, or hard to listen to
  • 2 = Poor: unnatural with noticeable artifacts
  • 3 = Fair: somewhat natural but with clear artifacts
  • 4 = Good: mostly natural with minor artifacts
  • 5 = Excellent: very natural and human-like

Please use headphones and complete the test in a quiet environment.
